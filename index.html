About Me

A brief introduction to who I am and what I do

Introduction

Currently, I focus on research and development in Artificial Intelligence, particularly in Computer Vision, Graphics, and Digital Image Processing. My work involves building and experimenting with models for image analysis, visual recognition, and real-time processing using frameworks such as PyTorch, OpenCV, and modern deep learning techniques.

In addition to AI research, I also have experience in web development, where I build full-stack applications and APIs using technologies such as React, Node.js, Express, and Flask. This background allows me to integrate intelligent vision systems into practical applications, combining backend engineering with interactive web interfaces.

My workflow focuses on writing clean, maintainable code, designing scalable systems, and applying computational methods to solve visual data problems. I am constantly exploring new approaches in AI and computer vision while developing tools and applications that bridge research and real-world software systems.

Key Skills:

  • Frameworks: Flask, Django, React.js, Next.js
  • Computer Vision & AI: PyTorch, OpenCV, Deep Learning, Image Processing
  • Web Development: Node.js, Express.js, RESTful APIs

  • DevOps & Tools: Docker, Git, CI/CD
